# Commercial Boundary

LidoPro is a proprietary commercial product for beach club management.

The current application is local-first and under active development. It is in pre-release / private commercial readiness, not a generally available customer release.

## Product Scope

Commercial capabilities may include:

- local operational beach management;
- Lido Studio layout and map design;
- booking and reservation workflows;
- customer records and account history;
- pricing, catalog, and tariff management;
- account ledgers and reporting;
- payment workflows;
- cloud sync and multi-device operation;
- customer-facing booking surfaces.

Only implemented and explicitly released features are available. Public source visibility is not a commercial license and does not authorize production or customer deployment.

## Non-Live Commercial Boundaries

Payment integrations, hosted cloud services, customer accounts, public booking portals, SaaS operation, and production authentication require separate implementation, provider setup, terms, privacy policy, security review, and release authorization.

References to Lido Pay, Lido Cloud, customer accounts, hosted booking, Satispay, PayPal, Stripe, or other payment/account providers are commercial product boundaries unless and until they are implemented, configured, and explicitly released.

## No Automatic Commercial Permission

Commercial pilots, private deployments, customer evaluations, reseller activity, agency delivery, white-label use, hosting, SaaS operation, or customer production use require written approval from Francesco Maiomascio.

No reseller, agency, white-label, hosting, SaaS, production customer deployment, or commercial operation rights are granted by this repository.
