# Public Commercial Repository Policy

LidoPro may be visible as a public/source-available repository, but it remains proprietary commercial software.

The repository is not open source and is not free software. Public visibility supports transparency, portfolio review, technical evaluation, and development reference only.

## Public Visibility Does Not Grant

Repository access does not grant:

- commercial use rights;
- production use rights;
- redistribution rights;
- hosting or SaaS rights;
- white-label rights;
- resale or agency delivery rights;
- customer deployment rights;
- payment/account/cloud operation rights;
- trademark, logo, icon, or brand rights;
- rights to misleading fork branding or confusingly similar product names.

## Required Public Hygiene

The repository must not contain:

- real customer data;
- real booking, account, payment, or business data;
- secrets, tokens, credentials, certificates, signing keys, or provider keys;
- local production databases;
- private backups or exports;
- release artifacts unless explicitly intended;
- misleading live cloud, payment, account, customer portal, or production-ready claims.

## Fork And Branding Boundary

Forks or mirrors must not present themselves as LidoPro, a LidoPro release, an official product, a commercial deployment, or an authorized derivative. External use of the LidoPro name, wordmark, siren/mermaid mark, screenshots, UI identity, or product copy requires written permission.
