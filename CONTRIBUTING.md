# Contributing

LidoPro is proprietary commercial software.

Public contributions are not broadly accepted unless explicitly invited by Francesco Maiomascio. Feedback may be accepted through issues or discussions if those channels are enabled.

Pull requests do not imply acceptance, ownership transfer approval, product rights, commercial rights, redistribution rights, or release inclusion.

## Contribution Rules

Do not submit contributions that contain:

- real customer data;
- private booking, account, payment, or business data;
- copied proprietary code;
- unclear third-party assets;
- licensed images without explicit rights;
- secrets, credentials, API keys, tokens, certificates, or signing material;
- generated databases or private backups.

Any accepted contribution must include contributor confirmation or assignment compatible with proprietary commercial software. Contributors must understand that accepted contributions do not grant ownership, brand rights, product rights, commercial rights, redistribution rights, hosting rights, or customer deployment rights.
